Microneedling with Exosomes

Microneedling with Exosomes is an advanced skin rejuvenation treatment designed to improve overall skin health, texture and glow.

The treatment combines microneedling with powerful exosome technology to support the skin's natural repair process and encourage collagen production.

Exosomes are packed with growth factors, peptides and skin-rejuvenating ingredients that help calm inflammation, support healing and improve the appearance of fine lines, acne scarring, pigmentation and uneven skin texture.

Ideal for: Healthier, brighter, firmer-looking skin with minimal downtime. Skin can appear smoother, more radiant and refreshed as collagen continues to rebuild over the following weeks.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you may want to know before booking

Helpful answers about qualifications, treatments, patch tests, preparation and aftercare.

What qualifications do you hold?

I am fully qualified and insured in all treatments offered at LP Laser Beauty Clinic. I hold recognised VTCT qualifications in Facial Electrical Treatments, Laser & Light Hair Removal, Skin Rejuvenation and Advanced Skin Treatments. With nearly 20 years of experience in healthcare and patient care, client safety and professionalism are at the heart of every treatment.

What is IPL Skin Rejuvenation?

IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) is a non-invasive treatment that uses light energy to target various skin concerns, including:

  • Sun damage
  • Age spots
  • Pigmentation
  • Rosacea
  • Thread veins
  • Acne
  • Uneven skin tone

IPL stimulates collagen production, helping to improve the overall appearance and texture of the skin.

Is IPL painful?

Most clients describe IPL as a mild snapping sensation, similar to an elastic band against the skin. Treatments are generally well tolerated and require little to no downtime.

How many IPL treatments will I need?

This depends on your skin concern and desired results. Most clients require a course of 3-6 treatments spaced several weeks apart for optimal results.

What is Microneedling?

Microneedling is an advanced skin treatment that uses tiny sterile needles to create controlled micro-injuries in the skin. This stimulates the body's natural healing response, increasing collagen and elastin production.

Microneedling can help improve:

  • Fine lines and wrinkles
  • Acne scarring
  • Enlarged pores
  • Uneven skin texture
  • Skin laxity
  • Pigmentation concerns
Does Microneedling hurt?

A topical numbing cream can be applied if required to maximise comfort. Most clients find the treatment very manageable and experience only mild discomfort.

How many Microneedling sessions will I need?

Many clients notice improvements after one treatment, however a course of 3-6 treatments is usually recommended for the best long-term results.

What is a patch test and why do I need one?

A patch test is a small test treatment carried out before Laser Hair Removal or IPL treatments.

It helps to:

  • Assess skin suitability
  • Check for any adverse reactions
  • Determine the safest treatment settings
  • Ensure your treatment is both safe and effective

Patch tests are required at least 24-48 hours before treatment.

Do I need a consultation before treatment?

Yes. Every new client receives a thorough consultation to discuss medical history, treatment suitability, expectations and aftercare requirements.

How should I prepare for Laser Hair Removal?

Before your appointment:

  • Shave the treatment area 24 hours before treatment
  • Avoid waxing, plucking or threading for at least 4 weeks
  • Avoid sun exposure and fake tan
  • Follow all pre-treatment advice provided during consultation
What aftercare is required following IPL or Microneedling?

After treatment you should:

  • Avoid direct sun exposure
  • Apply SPF 30-50 daily
  • Avoid heat treatments such as saunas and hot tubs
  • Avoid harsh skincare products
  • Follow the personalised aftercare advice provided

Protecting your skin after treatment is essential to achieve the best possible results.

Are your treatments suitable for all skin types?

Suitability varies depending on the treatment and your skin type. During your consultation, a full assessment will be carried out to determine the safest and most effective treatment plan for you.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book online, contact us via social media, email, or telephone. If you're unsure which treatment is right for you, we recommend booking a consultation first.
